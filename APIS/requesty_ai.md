# Requesty AI

Requesty AI is an AI model discovery and routing platform that provides access to 500+ AI models from providers like OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, AWS Bedrock, and Azure. It offers filtering by price, features, and includes free tier models.

## Pros

- Large selection of models from multiple providers
- Filtering options including free models
- Routing capabilities for LLM access

## Cons

- May require account setup
- Dependent on underlying provider availability
