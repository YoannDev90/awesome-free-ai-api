# TypeGPT

TypeGPT offers a unified API for 200+ AI models with a free Starter plan providing 1,000 credits per month and access to 50+ models.

## Resources

**Website**: <https://typegpt.ai/>

**Pricing**: <https://typegpt.ai/pricing>

**Models**: <https://typegpt.ai/models>

**Documentation**: <https://typegpt.ai/docs>

## Supported Models

Access to 200+ models including GPT-4o, Claude, Gemini, etc., with pay-per-use beyond free credits.

## Pricing

- **Starter (Free)**: 1,000 credits/month, 50+ models
- **Pro ($29/mo)**: 50,000 credits/month, 150+ models
- **Enterprise**: Custom
