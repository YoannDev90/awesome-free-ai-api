# Infip

Infip provides free access to various AI image generation models, including Flux, SDXL, Imagen, and more, with no cost for usage.

## Resources

**Website**: <https://infip.pro/>

**Models**: <https://infip.pro/models>

**Pricing**: <https://infip.pro/plans>

## Supported Models

All models are free:
- Z-Image-Turbo
- Nano Banana Pro (marked Pro but free?)
- Nano Banana
- Imagen 3
- Imagen 4
- Qwen
- Flux Schnell
- Flux 2 Klein 9B
- Flux 2 Klein 4B
- Flux 2 Dev
- Lucid Origin
- Phoenix
- SDXL
- SDXL Lite
- Dreamshaper
