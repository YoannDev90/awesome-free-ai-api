# AIHubMix

AIHubMix is a unified API gateway providing access to 600+ AI models from various providers, including many free-tier models with usage limits.

## Resources

**Website**: <https://aihubmix.com/>

**Models**: <https://aihubmix.com/models>

**Documentation**: <https://docs.aihubmix.com/>

## Supported Models

Includes free versions of models like:
- step-3.5-flash-free
- gemini-3-flash-preview-free
- glm-4.7-flash-free
- And many others with limits (e.g., 5 req/min, 250-500 req/day, token quotas)

Full list available on their models page.

## Pricing

- Free tiers for select models with rate limits
- Paid models with per-token pricing

## Pros

Huge selection of models

Many free options

## Cons

Usage limits on free models
