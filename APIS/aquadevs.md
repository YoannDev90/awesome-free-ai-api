# AquaDevs

AquaDevs offers a free Surface plan with access to 14+ text models and 4+ image models, along with URL extraction.

## Resources

**Website**: <https://aquadevs.com/>

**Plans**: <https://aquadevs.com/plan>

## Supported Models

- 14+ text models
- 4+ image models

## Pricing

- **Surface (Free)**: 14+ text models, 4+ image models, 10 req/min, up to 2 subdomains
- **Current**: Custom pricing
- **Abyss**: Fully customized

## Pros

Free access to multiple models

## Cons

Limited requests per minute
