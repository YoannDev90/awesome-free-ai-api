# Mixedbread

Mixedbread offers AI-powered tools for embeddings, reranking, search, and document parsing with a free Basic plan including monthly usage allocations.

## Resources

**Website**: <https://www.mixedbread.com/>

**Pricing**: <https://www.mixedbread.com/pricing>

**Documentation**: <https://www.mixedbread.com/docs>

## Supported Features

- Embeddings: Text, documents, images, video, audio
- Reranking
- Search
- Document parsing

## Pricing

- **Basic (Free)**: 2M tokens ingestion/month, 100 queries/month, 200 pages parsing/month, 3 stores
- **Scale ($20/mo)**: Unlimited usage with pay-as-you-go
- **Enterprise**: Custom pricing

## Pros

Free tier available

Wide range of AI-powered features

## Cons

Focused on search and embeddings, not general LLM inference
